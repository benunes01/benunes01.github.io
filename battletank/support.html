Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pause the game?

Tap the "PAUSE" button between the D-pad and fire button at the bottom of the screen. From there you can resume, restart, or quit the level.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings (from the main menu) and tap the language button. The game supports 8 languages: English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, and Russian.

How do I unlock new tanks?

Earn coins by completing levels and defeating enemies. Visit the Garage from the main menu to unlock and upgrade tanks.

How do I get 3 stars?

Complete the level without dying. 3 stars = zero deaths, 2 stars = one death, 1 star = two or more deaths.

Does the game work offline?

Yes! Tank Battle: Classic War works 100% offline. No internet connection is required to play.

How do I restore my purchase?

If you previously purchased "Remove Ads", go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchase" to restore your ad-free status.

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping the app installed.
