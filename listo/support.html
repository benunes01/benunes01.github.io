Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a shopping list?

Tap the + button on the home screen, give your list a name, optionally set a budget, and start adding items.

Can I reuse a completed list?

Yes! Long-press any completed list and tap "Duplicate". A new copy will be created with all items unchecked.

Does Listo require internet?

No. Listo works 100% offline. All your data is stored locally on your device.

How do I change the language?

Listo automatically detects your device language. It supports 9 languages: English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, and Hindi.

Is my data safe?

Your data never leaves your device. No account, no cloud, no data collection. See our Privacy Policy.
