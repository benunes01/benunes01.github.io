Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Becaptain Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Tank Battle: Classic War mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Advertising Data

Our App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • App usage data (session duration, interactions with ads)

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

1.2 Purchase Data

Our App offers an in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Purchases are processed through RevenueCat, which may collect:

  • Anonymous app user ID
  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Device platform and app version

We do not have access to your payment information (credit card, billing address). All payments are processed securely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

1.3 Game Data

Your game progress (levels completed, coins earned, tank upgrades) is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any gameplay data on external servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Advertising: To display relevant ads and measure ad performance
  • Purchases: To verify and manage your ad-free purchase status across devices
  • App improvement: Aggregated, anonymous data may be used to improve the App

3. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Advertising
  • RevenueCat — Purchase management
  • Apple App Store — Payment processing (iOS)
  • Google Play Store — Payment processing (Android)

4. Your Choices

4.1 Ad Personalization

You can opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Tracking, and disable tracking for this App
  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

4.2 Remove Ads

You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option within the App to permanently disable all advertisements. This is a one-time, non-consumable purchase.

4.3 Data Deletion

Since we do not store personal data on our servers, uninstalling the App removes all locally stored game data. To reset your advertising identifier, use your device's privacy settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through our App. All purchase transactions are processed through secure, encrypted channels provided by Apple and Google.

7. International Users

7.1 European Economic Area (EEA)

If you are located in the EEA, you will be presented with a consent dialog before personalized ads are shown, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You may choose to receive only non-personalized ads.

7.2 California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know what personal information is collected and to request its deletion. Contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
