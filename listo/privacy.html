Privacy Policy

Listo: Shopping List — Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Listo is built with privacy as a core principle. The app works entirely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

Listo does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login required
  • No personal data collected (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • No location data collected
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs
  • No data shared with third parties

Local Data Storage

All data you create in Listo (shopping lists, items, budgets, history) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data is never transmitted to any server.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Advertising

Listo displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information for the purpose of serving ads, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

On iOS, Listo respects your App Tracking Transparency (ATT) preferences. If you decline tracking, only contextual (non-personalized) ads will be shown.

Children's Privacy

Listo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
